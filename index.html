This course explores Generative Art & Algorithmic Composition. Generative Art is a practice where the creator makes a set of instructions for a system to execute. The artist creates a system, nurtures it, and refines it, then lets the computer program generate the final resulting visuals. Algorithmic Composition is an active field of research which marries the fields of computer science and music composition to design computer systems that compose music.
